ABOUT US


An online test platform is a web-based tool that allows users to create, manage, and grade exams online. It offers remote testing, minimizing the need for a teacher's physical presence, making it convenient for students and instructors to assess students regardless of their location. Reports about student performance are detailed, giving valuable insights to educators and administrators, identifying areas to improve teaching approaches. Online test platforms are cost-effective, and offer immediate feedback to students, improving their subject knowledge. Educators can also upload question banks, select random questions, and customize grading schemes, providing customized feedback to students.
